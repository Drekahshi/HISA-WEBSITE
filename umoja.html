The Three Critical Bottlenecks

The Liquidity Freeze

Trillions of shillings worth of value locked in illiquid assets - land, real estate, and small business equity that cannot be converted to productive capital.

The Access Fortress

A two-tier financial system where sophisticated investment products remain exclusive to wealthy individuals while 80% of Kenyans are limited to basic savings.

The Trust Vacuum

Opaque financial systems and corruption scandals that drive citizens away from formal investment channels, keeping capital under mattresses.
